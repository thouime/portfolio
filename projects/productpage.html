Blueberry Pie

Ingredients:

  • 4 Cups of Fresh Blueberries
  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  • 3/4 Cup of White Sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons of Cornstarch
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoons of Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 Recipe Pastry for 9 Inch Double Crust Pie

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 Degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix sugar, cornstarch, salt, and cinnamon, and sprinkle over blueberries.
  3. Line pie dish with one pie crust. Pour berry mixture into the crust, and dot with butter. Cut remaining pastry into 1/2 - 3/4 inch wide strips, and make lattice top. Crimp and flute edges.
  4. Bake pie on lower shelf of oven for about 50 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
  5. Eat the pie!

Banana Pie

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 2 cups milk
  • ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 3 egg yolks, beaten
  • 4 bananas, sliced
  • ¼ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 (9 inch) baked pastry shell, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the sugar, flour, and salt. Add milk in gradually while stirring gently. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture is bubbly. Keep stirring and cook for about 2 more minutes, and then remove from the burner.
  2. Stir a small quantity of the hot mixture into the beaten egg yolks, and immediately add egg yolk mixture to the rest of the hot mixture. Cook for 2 more minutes; remember to keep stirring. Remove the mixture from the stove, and add butter and vanilla. Stir until the whole thing has a smooth consistency.
  3. Slice bananas into the cooled baked pastry shell. Top with pudding mixture.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 12 to 15 minutes. Chill for an hour.
  5. Enjoy a delicious pie.

Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
  • 1 (14 ounce) can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 large eggs large eggs
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Whisk pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt in medium bowl until smooth. Pour into crust. Bake 15 minutes.
  2. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F and continue baking 35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted 1 inch from crust comes out clean. Cool. Garnish as desired. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
